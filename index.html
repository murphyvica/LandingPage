About the Price Prediction Model

Our price prediction model addresses the common problem of uncertainty in determining the best time to purchase a product. To solve this issue, we developed a Machine Learning model that leverages thousands of historical price data points to accurately predict the price of a product in the next two weeks. Our model provides graphical price predictions that enable individuals to make informed purchasing decisions, leading to more effective allocation of resources. By utilizing our Price Prediction Model, individuals can avoid overspending, and optimize their purchasing decisions. Our project demonstrates the utility of data analytics in bettering everyday finances.

Problem Statement

How might we empower users of NetPrism so that they may make informed decisions of when to buy a product online?
